Welcome to the Arithmoi Library

An initiative of The Arithmoi Foundation, this library is dedicated to resources supporting the study of mathematics, physics, and metaphysics from an ontological standpoint. Explore fundamental questions about the nature of reality through our curated collection.

Our digital shelves offer access to texts, articles, and other materials for those engaged in rigorous mathematical and philosophical inquiry.
